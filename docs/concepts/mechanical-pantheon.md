# Mechanical Pantheon

A way to think about “AI systems” as a **set of roles** rather than a single god-model.

Instead of one monolith, imagine a pantheon of specialized, accountable agents:
- **Sentinel**: watches for anomalies + abuse
- **Archivist**: preserves memory + provenance
- **Operator**: executes playbooks safely
- **Mediator**: helps humans resolve conflicts
- **Builder**: ships tools and infrastructure

## Why this matters
Specialization + clear interfaces makes systems:
- easier to audit
- easier to replace
- less likely to fail catastrophically

It’s a governance idea disguised as a metaphor.
