# Economic Sustainability

“Sustainability” here is not vibes. It’s whether a system can keep functioning when:
- demand spikes
- supply chains break
- politics turns hostile
- your best people leave

## The core question
What is the **minimal, steady-state funding + maintenance loop** that keeps a capability alive?

## What to look for
- Products/services where maintenance is naturally funded (not an afterthought)
- Incentives aligned with uptime + safety (not growth at any cost)
- Tooling that reduces operational burden (automation that *decreases* fragility)

## Why it matters for AI
Agent systems that depend on a brittle funding model (or hidden subsidies) will fail exactly when we need them most.
