# Community-Owned Infrastructure

A “happy path” future needs communities that can *operate* the basics without begging permission from distant monopolies.

## What counts as infrastructure
- Energy generation + storage (and the expertise to maintain it)
- Connectivity (fiber, mesh, satellite, local networking)
- Water, sanitation, shelter
- Local fabrication + repair capacity (tools, parts, test rigs)
- Compute (on-device, local clusters, and accountable cloud)

## The point
Infrastructure isn’t just “utilities” — it’s **agency**. If you can’t keep the lights on, you can’t keep your institutions coherent.

## Investment primitives
- Open standards + interoperable hardware
- Maintenance-first design (Lindy: boring wins)
- Local operator toolchains (docs, training, spare parts)
- Auditable automation (robots/agents that are inspectable and safe-by-default)
