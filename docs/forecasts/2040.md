# 2040 — ASI Era

A scenario where systems are not just “smart,” but operate with sustained strategic advantage in many domains.

## Happy-path focus
- Institutions become more legible (auditable, explainable governance)
- Rights and agency are protected by infrastructure, not vibes
- Abundance is real enough to defang zero-sum politics

## Boundary markers
- Closed-source sovereign ASI arms race
- Human autonomy reduced to managed consumption

## What to watch
- Compute/energy concentration trends
- Governance experiments that scale without coercion
- The emergence (or absence) of verifiable safety layers
