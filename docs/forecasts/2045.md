# 2045 — Singularity

“Singularity” here means the future becomes hard to predict because the rate of capability improvement is too high.

## Happy-path framing
The goal isn’t to control everything. It’s to ensure:
- humans retain agency and dignity
- systems remain auditable and corrigible
- power doesn’t collapse into a single irreversible attractor

## Boundary markers
- One-way doors everywhere (irreversible deployments)
- Value lock-in by narrow coalitions

## Strategic posture
- Build reversible systems
- Prefer modularity + replaceability
- Invest in tools that widen participation in building the physical world
