# 2030 — AGI Era

A plausible near-term world where general-purpose models are strong enough to automate large fractions of knowledge work.

## Happy-path focus
- Alignment bandwidth increases (tools, interfaces, verification)
- Education and retraining are treated as infrastructure
- Energy + compute become explicit policy domains

## Boundary markers (20%)
- Mass persuasion + automated fraud becomes industrial
- “Safety theater” replaces real verification

## What we should build now
- Agent supply-chain security (signed skills, permission manifests)
- Human-usable cryptography + identity
- Local resilience: repair, fabrication, and community ops
