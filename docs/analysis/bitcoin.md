# Bitcoin

## Why it matters (for the happy path)
- Hard money as a coordination primitive (especially when institutions erode)
- Censorship resistance, with real tradeoffs (privacy, UX, governance)

## Questions we track
- What L2s and custody models actually work for normal humans?
- Where do mining/energy dynamics intersect with grid resilience?
- What does "Bitcoin as settlement" look like when agents transact?
