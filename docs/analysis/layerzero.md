# LayerZero

Interoperability is either the bridge to composability—or the bridge to contagion.

## Why it matters
- Cross-chain messaging is a coordination force multiplier
- It’s also historically a top source of catastrophic exploits

## Questions we track
- What trust assumptions are actually being made?
- How do audits + verification scale?
- Can we design “blast-radius-limited” messaging?
